About Me

I am currently a student at Northeastern University (NEU), China. My research focuses on robotics and embodied AI, with a particular interest in combining vision and control for manipulation tasks.

Research Interests

  • Mobile Manipulation (移动操作): Whole-body control and planning for mobile manipulators.
  • Humanoid Robots (人形机器人): Teleoperation and policy learning for humanoid systems.
  • Robot Learning & Control: Deep learning for dynamic locomotion, manipulation, and adaptive control.
